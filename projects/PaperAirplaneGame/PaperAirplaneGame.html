Paper Airplane Game

How To Play

Steer the paper airplane down the obstacle for
as long as you can. The further down you reach,
the more points you will get. The quickest time
to get the most points is also recorded.

Every 50 points you get results in ascending to the
next level. Every level increase means the horizontal
walls get longer, thus requiring you to make steeper
turns to get through.

Note that the more downward the paper airplane faces,
the faster it falls. This can be used to get points
quicker, but it's harder to control the plane in such
a way.

Controls

Left Key - Steers the paper airplane to the left of
the screen. It is preferable to hold the key
for steering.

Right Key - Steers the paper airplane to the right of
the screen. It is preferable to hold the key
for steering.

Other Notes

You can turn the music off/on with the "On/Off" button on
the bottom-right of the game screen. This doesn't affect sound
effects, though.
